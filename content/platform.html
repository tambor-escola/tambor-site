The Enterprise Intelligence Platform suite

Seven components, one platform.

Seven integrated components, each purpose-built to support the way governments and programmes actually work with data.

DH

DHIS2 Enterprise

Enterprise-grade DHIS2 hosting with development, test, and production environments, automated version patching, upgrade assistance, and application monitoring built in.

AP

Analytics Platform

Integrates data from DHIS2 and multiple other sources into a unified warehouse, with no-code connectors, predictive analytics, and Python and R scripting support.

SB

Super BI

Flexible dashboards and data visualisation embedded directly inside DHIS2, powered by Apache Superset with near real-time data availability and direct warehouse querying.

AI

Convo AI

Ask questions of your data in plain language and receive charts, tables, and automated interpretations in seconds. No SQL knowledge or analyst required.

DA

Dharma

Cloud-based mobile-first data collection for field surveys, emergency response, and programme monitoring. Non-technical staff design and deploy forms in minutes, with automatic sync when connectivity is available.

AS

Advanced Security

AWS Web Application Firewall, vulnerability scanning, DAST reporting, and VPN access, with compliance add-ons and full documentation and audit support.

IN

Standards & Interoperability

Built on open standards including HL7, FHIR, and OpenHIE, ensuring compatibility with existing national health information architecture and future integrations.
